# Human Employment Manifesto

## Our Commitment

Sovereign Dominion exists to **multiply human skill**, not replace it. Every feature we build must answer this question: *Does this create more work for skilled tradespeople, or less?*

## Design Principles

### 1. Technology as Amplifier, Not Replacement
- AR design tools let homeowners communicate better with contractors
- AI compliance checking helps workers avoid costly rework
- Voice commands reduce paperwork so workers spend more time building

### 2. The Skill Credential System
- Workers earn verifiable, portable credentials on the blockchain
- Credentials travel with the worker, not the employer
- Apprentice → Journeyman → Master progression is recognized industry-wide

### 3. Transparent Pricing Benefits Workers
- Real-time supplier pricing gives small contractors competitive intelligence
- Change order blockchain creates tamper-proof records protecting workers
- Escrow smart contracts ensure workers get paid for completed work

### 4. Training as Investment
- AR/VR safety training in 10 languages reduces accidents
- Gamified skill-building modules accessible to all literacy levels
- Certifications recognized by insurance and licensing boards

## Metrics We Track

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Jobs posted via platform | Growing QoQ |
| Worker credentials issued | 10,000+ year 1 |
| Average contractor revenue lift | +23% |
| Training completion rate | >85% |
| Workplace incidents (trained users) | -40% vs baseline |

## What We Refuse to Build

- Automated bidding that races wages to the bottom
- "Gig economy" features that eliminate benefits
- Surveillance tools that track worker productivity
- AI systems that replace skilled judgment with automation

## The Economy We're Building

A world where the most skilled tradespeople earn what they deserve, where small contractors can compete with large firms, where new workers have a clear path to mastery, and where technology makes hard work safer and more rewarding.
